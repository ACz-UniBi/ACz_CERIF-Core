# Date

Represents dates of the Gregorian calendar in line with the ISO 8601:2019 standard.<sup>[1](#fn1)</sup>

## Variants

Three granularities are supported:
1. Full date (YYYY-MM-DD)
2. Year and month (YYYY-MM)
3. Just the year (YYYY)
