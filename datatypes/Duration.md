# Duration

## Definition
Represents durations in line with the ISO 8601:2019 standard.<sup>[1](#fn1)</sup>

## Notes
Watch the syntax: `P4M` means four months, whereas `PT15M` means fifteen minutes.

---
## Matches
Closely matches `xsd:duration`.<sup>[2](#fn2)</sup>
